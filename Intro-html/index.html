Bear News

August, 2021


This is a picture of four cute black bear cubs that were part of an urban/suburban bear study in Asheville. The study found:

  • Black bears in Asheville are twice as big as their rural counterparts
  • Asheville black bears are having cubs at half the age of their rural counterparts
